# AirThings

| | |
| --- | --- |
| ***Website*** | https://www.airthings.com/business |
| ***Category*** | IAQ Monitoring |


## About

AirThings provide Indoor Air Quality and Radon measurement IoT devices and business API. There are a variety of models with a mix of different sensors.

## Modelling Guidance

### Relationships
| *Relationship* | *Required* | *Description* |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `brick:hasLocation` | `true` | AirThings IoT devices are installed directly in the zone of interest. Providing a `location` for device is required to integrate it into the building model. | 

### Structure

No `parts` required.
These are simple devices that contain a set of points.
